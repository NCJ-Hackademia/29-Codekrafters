Getting Started Guide

Welcome to Paraso, a professional-grade gesture and voice control system powered by adaptive AI. Control your computer completely hands-free with natural movements and voice commands.

Quick Start Guide

1

Gesture Recognition

Click Start Gesture Recognition to activate the camera and begin detecting your hand gestures. Perform the trained gestures to trigger keyboard actions.

2

Mouse Control

Use Start Mouse Controller to control your cursor with hand movements. Move your hand to position the cursor, and make specific gestures for clicks.

3

Voice Commands

Click Start Voice Commands to control your computer with voice. Say commands like "up", "down", "left", "right" to navigate.

4

Voice Typing

Activate Start Voice Typing, then click in any text field (like Notepad) and speak clearly. Your speech will be converted to text automatically.

When finished, click Stop All Processes to close all running components and free up system resources.

How to Use

  1. Train the default gestures or add your own custom gestures
  2. Customize key mappings by clicking the "Map Key" button on any command
  3. Click "Train New Gesture" to record your gestures
  4. Once training is complete, click "Live Prediction" to start
  5. Perform the gestures in front of your camera to trigger the mapped keys
